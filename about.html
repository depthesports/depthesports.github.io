Dive to Victory

Depth Esports is a competitive and content esports organization. Depth found early success in Fortnite, and it has been part of our foundation since the beginning. Since then, we have expanded to multiple titles such as but not limited to: Rainbow Six Siege and Smash Brothers Ultimate. Located in the US, we have members globally. Founded on May 25th, 2018 by Jonathan "Levitee" Green; cofounded by Ian Cusick. Our ultimate goal as an organization is to become a leader. We want to field the best teams, create the best content, and have the best branding. We're going to change the game.
